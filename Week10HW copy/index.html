Unidentified Flying Objects (UFOs)

UFOs, or Unidentified Flying Objects, refer to any aerial phenomena or objects in the sky that cannot be readily identified or explained by conventional means. They are often associated with reports of sightings of unexplained or mysterious flying craft and have been the subject of speculation, investigation, and debate regarding their origin and nature.

Military Surveillance

Military surveillance encompasses the systematic monitoring and observation activities conducted by armed forces to gather intelligence, maintain situational awareness, and enhance security through various technologies and methods, including satellites, drones, reconnaissance aircraft, and ground-based sensors.

UFOs can come under military surveillance when they are detected or reported within a military's operational area or airspace. Military organizations have radar systems, sensors, and personnel trained to monitor and investigate aerial threats or anomalies, which may include UFO sightings.
